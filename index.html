About Us


AZeotropy is a two-day annual Chemical Engineering Symposium organized by the Chemical Engineering Department, IIT Bombay. We are a non-profit organization striving to bridge the gap between students, industry, and academia, and promoting it at a national level. The symposium witnesses participation from 300+ colleges from all over India, 10+ corporate houses and 50+ industrialists of the field.

We, at AZeotropy, give you a chance to showcase your leadership and organizational skills through our CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM. A Campus Ambassador is the representative of AZeotropy in their respective colleges and his/her responsibility is to connect with the students who are enthusiastic to participate in the events and competitions conducted by AZeotropy. We are inviting registrations of students from colleges with programs such as chemical, petrochemical, and petroleum engineering.
